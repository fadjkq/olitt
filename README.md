## Who Is Olitt Actually For?

The platform markets itself at local businesses specifically — salons, plumbers, dentists, restaurants, law firms, construction companies. But the user base is broader than that.

If you're a **freelancer or solopreneur** who needs a portfolio or landing page and doesn't want to pay Squarespace $23/month indefinitely, Olitt's pricing is almost embarrassingly good.

If you're a **small business owner** who knows you need to be on Google Maps but hasn't touched your Google Business Profile in months, LocalForce is targeted exactly at that problem.

If you're a **developer or agency** who builds client sites, the WordPress hosting at $9.99/year as a base and the ability to inject custom CSS and JavaScript means you can use Olitt's AI scaffold and then customize it fully.

Where Olitt is *less* ideal: complex enterprise sites, heavy e-commerce with intricate inventory systems, or anyone who needs deep third-party integrations. The platform's strength is speed and price, not infinite customization.
